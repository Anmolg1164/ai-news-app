# **App Name**: Dharma Navigator

## Core Features:

- Bottom Navigation Bar: A pill-shaped floating navbar at the bottom of the screen with a glassmorphism effect for core app navigation.
- Travel Intelligence Panel: A dedicated side panel on the left displaying curated travel intelligence and insights.
- Gita Wisdom Card: A prominent card on the left displaying daily or random verses of wisdom from the Bhagavad Gita.
- News Briefs Storage: Integration with Firestore to store and manage a collection of travel-related 'news_briefs'.
- News Briefs Display: Fetch and display the latest travel news briefs from the Firestore 'news_briefs' collection.
- Gita Wisdom Interpretation Tool: Utilize generative AI to provide clear and concise interpretations or modern relevance for the displayed Gita wisdom verses.

## Style Guidelines:

- Color Scheme: A light color scheme evoking serenity, wisdom, and the clarity of a journey. The core concepts guiding this choice are 'Journey, Wisdom, Serenity'.
- Primary Color: A deep, calming blue (#2C5F8A). This color suggests professionalism, stability, and depth, aligning with both intelligence and profound wisdom.
- Background Color: A very light, desaturated greyish-blue (#E2E8ED). This provides a clean, open, and serene canvas that complements the primary color.
- Accent Color: A vibrant yet refreshing cyan (#8CE0E0). This analogous color provides a dynamic highlight for interactive elements and calls to action, symbolizing discovery and clarity.
- Headline Font: 'Space Grotesk' (sans-serif), for a modern, precise, and tech-forward feel suitable for 'Travel Intelligence' content. 
- Body Font: 'Inter' (sans-serif), used for general body text and 'Gita Wisdom' content, providing excellent readability with a neutral, objective aesthetic.
- Adopt a clean, minimalist line-art or filled icon style for elements in the bottom navbar, side panel, and content cards. Icons should visually represent travel, navigation, and wisdom motifs.
- A distinct layout featuring a fixed, floating pill-shaped navigation bar at the bottom, and a dynamic left-aligned section for 'Travel Intelligence' and 'Gita Wisdom', adjacent to a primary content area.
- Implement subtle animations, especially for the glassmorphism effect on the navbar (backdrop-blur-md) and smooth transitions for opening/closing the side panel or navigating between sections.
